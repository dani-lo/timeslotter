Timeslots Booker - with Doctor Z

About this app

This is a simple time slots booking application. Functionality is limited to viewing existing bookings and creating new ones

Please book here your timeslots with Doctor Z

You can book any amount of time, Dr Z doesnt mind he's got plenty of time. Just remember after creating the slot you can not change it, that's it, if you make a mistake you will need to create a new timeslot sequence.
